<p><ul><li>Prime Lenses</li><li>Zoom Lenses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital cine lenses market primarily consists of two types: prime lenses and zoom lenses. Prime lenses have a fixed focal length, offering superior optical quality and wider apertures, making them ideal for low-light conditions and creative depth of field. In contrast, zoom lenses provide variable focal lengths, offering versatility for filmmakers to quickly adjust framing without changing lenses. Both types cater to different filming styles and requirements, driving preferences in the cinematography industry based on creative needs and production specifics.</p></p>
