<p><p>The Automatic Banknote Sorter market is divided into two main types: fully automatic and semi-automatic systems. This type enhances efficiency and accuracy, ideal for high-volume operations. Conversely, semi-automatic sorters require some level of human input, facilitating limited automation while still boosting operational speed and sorting accuracy. Organizations often choose between these types based on their workflow needs and budget constraints.</p></p>
