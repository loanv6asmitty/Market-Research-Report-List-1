<p><p>The gyroplane engines market consists primarily of two types: 2-stroke and 4-stroke engines. 2-stroke engines are lightweight and simpler in design, offering higher power-to-weight ratios, making them ideal for recreational gyroplanes. However, they tend to be less fuel-efficient and produce more emissions. In contrast, 4-stroke engines, while heavier and more complex, provide greater fuel efficiency, lower emissions, and smoother operation, making them suitable for larger or more professional-grade gyroplanes. Both types cater to different needs within the market.</p></p>
