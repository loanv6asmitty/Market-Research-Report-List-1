<p><p>The semiconductor grade ion exchange resins market is primarily categorized into two types: anion ion-exchange resins and cation ion-exchange resins. Anion resins are designed to remove negatively charged ions, effectively purifying the water used in semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, cation resins target positively charged ions for similar purification purposes. Both types are crucial in achieving the high purity levels required for semiconductor processes, ensuring the reliability and efficiency of electronic components. Their applications are vital in advanced technology sectors.</p></p>
