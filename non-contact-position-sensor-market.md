<p><p>The non-contact position sensor market is categorized into two primary types: linear position sensors and rotary position sensors. Linear position sensors detect displacement along a straight path, commonly used in automation and manufacturing, ensuring precise measurements. Conversely, rotary position sensors measure the angular position of an object, essential for applications like robotics and motor control. Both types leverage technologies such as magnetic, capacitive, and optical sensing, providing reliable performance without physical contact, enhancing durability and reducing wear and tear.</p></p>
