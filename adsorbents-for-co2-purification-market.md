<p><p>The CO2 purification market features three primary adsorbent types: carbon-based adsorbents, zeolites, and metal oxide sorbents. Carbon-based adsorbents, such as activated carbon and biochar, are highly porous and effective in capturing CO2 through physical adsorption. Zeolites, crystalline aluminosilicates, offer selective adsorption due to their porous structure and cation exchange properties. Metal oxide sorbents leverage chemical interactions for enhanced CO2 capture, operating effectively at various temperatures. Each type plays a crucial role in achieving efficient CO2 removal and environmental sustainability.</p></p>
