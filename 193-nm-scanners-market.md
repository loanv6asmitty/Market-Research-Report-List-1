<p><p>The 193 nm scanners market is divided into two primary types: 193 nm dry scanners and 193 nm wet scanners. Dry scanners utilize a dry photoresist process, providing simpler handling and faster throughput, making them suitable for high-volume production. In contrast, wet scanners employ a wet photoresist process, allowing for better resolution and finer feature definitions, ideal for advanced semiconductor manufacturing. Both types play crucial roles in the lithography stage of chip production, addressing varying needs in the industry.</p></p>
