<p><ul><li>PE Prepainted</li><li>HDP Prepainted</li><li>SMP Prepainted</li><li>PVDF Prepainted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The prepainted steel coil market includes various types based on coatings. PE (Polyester) prepainted steel offers good weather resistance and is cost-effective for general applications. HDP (High-Durability Polyester) prepainted provides enhanced durability and UV resistance, suitable for more demanding environments. SMP (Silicone Modified Polyester) prepainted combines durability with a glossy finish, ideal for architectural applications. PVDF (Polyvinylidene Fluoride) prepainted steel delivers superior chemical resistance and is commonly used in high-end facades and industrial applications. Each type caters to specific performance and aesthetic requirements.</p></p>
