<p><p>The Rubber Injection Machinery market primarily consists of two types: vertical and horizontal machines. Vertical type machines are designed for space efficiency, allowing for easy loading and unloading of molds from above, often used for smaller parts. In contrast, horizontal type machines provide more accessibility and are ideal for larger molds, facilitating faster production cycles and automation. Both types serve unique applications in rubber processing, catering to varying production needs and operational layouts within manufacturing facilities.</p></p>
